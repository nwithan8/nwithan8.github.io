New Media Capstone

For my final course in the New Media Institute, I worked with a team to develop a mingolf game for the Microsoft Hololens. Specifically, I worked closely with Brad, the lead developer, as a liason between the coding and the other team members overseeing project management aspects. I made sure that an idea from one of the other teammates was possible for Brad to accomplish, and translated what Brad was doing back to the rest of the team. I also assisted in beta testing and resolving technical issues.

I also headed up the design and management of the project's website.
